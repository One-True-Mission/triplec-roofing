What We Do

Roofing Services

Everything Triple C Roofing brings to your home, from the first inspection through a finished, fully cleaned-up job site.

Roof Replacement

Complete tear-off and replacement using premium shingles and materials, installed by experienced crews and built to protect your home for years.

Roof Repair

Leaks, missing shingles, and wind damage handled fast and done right, so a small issue today does not become an expensive one tomorrow.

Storm & Hail Restoration

North Texas storms are hard on roofs. We inspect, document, and fully restore hail and wind damage to get your roof back to where it should be.

Free Roof Inspections

We inspect every roof, document what we find, and give you honest answers with no pressure. You will always know exactly where your roof stands.

Insurance Claim Assistance

We work directly with your insurance company to document damage and secure the full scope you are owed, making the claims process simple.

Roof Maintenance

Routine tune-ups, sealant checks, and minor fixes that extend the life of your roof and keep it performing season after season.
