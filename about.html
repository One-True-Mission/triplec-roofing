Our Story

Built On Integrity. Backed By Experience.

Triple C Roofing is a locally owned and operated company serving Fort Worth, Weatherford, and the greater DFW area. Owner Chris Sarchet was born and raised in Fort Worth and named the company after his two sons, Carter and Cannon, with the third C being himself.

Chris got his start in roofing in 2015 as a project manager, learned the trade quickly, and set sales records as a general manager at an established company before branching out on his own. He built Triple C to bring a good, trustworthy name back to roofing, because he saw too many homeowners who simply didn't trust roofers.

That mission still drives us. We treat every home like it's our own, deliver honest communication and clean job sites, and make absolutely sure you get the full scope you are owed when working with your insurance. Chris hopes his two sons carry on the Triple C legacy for many years to come.
